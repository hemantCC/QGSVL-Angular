Private Lease

Private Lease is a long-term rental option that allows individuals to rent a new car for a specified period of time and mileage. The private leasing formula includes all vehicle-related services for a single fixed monthly amount (insurance , maintenance and repairs , winter and summer tyres , breakdown and roadside assistance , road tax , immediate replacement vehicle).
